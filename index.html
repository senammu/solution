dosa

Dosa is a type of pancake made from a fermented batter. It is not a crepe. Its main ingredients are rice and black gram. Dosa is a typical part of the South Indian diet and popular all over the Indian subcontinent.

BIRIYANI

Biryani also known as biriyani or biriani, is a South Asian mixed rice dish with its origins among the Muslims of the Medieval Indian subcontinent.It is popular throughout the subcontinent and among the diaspora from the region.

Bisi bele bhath

Bisi bele is a rice-based dish with origins in the state of Karnataka, India.The traditional preparation of this dish is quite elaborate and involves the use of spicy masala, toor dal (a type of lentil) and vegetables.
